About Banglarbhumi

Banglarbhumi is an official online land records portal of West Bengal, designed to provide access to property information, land maps, mutation status, and other land-related services. It enables residents to access records digitally without visiting offices.

Users can check property details, view land maps, download certified copies, and track mutations from anywhere with internet access.

Top Features of the Portal

Land Record Search

Check owner information, land details, and usufruct records.

Map View Facility

View the official land maps for accurate locations and boundaries.

Mutation Status

Check the status of mutation requests online in real time.

How to Use the Portal

  1. Open the official website using the button above or visit Banglarbhumi.
  2. Select the service: Land Record, Mutation Status, Map View, etc.
  3. Enter required details like survey number, owner name, or district.
  4. Submit and view your land information instantly.
